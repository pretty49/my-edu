My Story & Mission

Hi, I'm Halima Masud!

Like many of you, my journey into web development was filled with excitement, but also a lot of confusion. I spent countless hours sifting through tutorials, trying to figure out which resources were reliable and what path I should follow. It often felt like I was trying to navigate a vast ocean without a compass.

That's why I created DevHub. My mission is to provide that compass for aspiring developers everywhere. I've curated the platforms and tools that personally helped me succeed, and I've built this space to offer the clear guidance I wish I had when I was starting.

This isn't just a list of links; it's a starting point for your career. My goal is to help you learn more efficiently, build your confidence, and join a global community of developers. Let's build the future of the web together.
